Coconut wax candles

Product Description:

Coconut Wax Candles are eco-friendly, clean-burning candles made from natural coconut wax. They provide a longer, even burn with minimal soot and smoke. Known for their excellent scent throw, these candles enhance any space with a calming aroma. Biodegradable and toxin-free, they are a sustainable alternative to traditional paraffin candles!

Product Features:

  • Eco-Friendly & Sustainable :Made from natural, biodegradable coconut wax.
  • Clean-Burning : Produces minimal soot and smoke for a healthier environment.
  • Longer Burn Time:Burns slower and more evenly than paraffin or soy candles.
  • Strong Scent Throw :Enhances spaces with a rich, lasting fragrance.
  • Non-Toxic & Chemical-Free:Free from harmful additives, making it safe for indoor use.
  • Smooth & Creamy Texture : Provides a luxurious, aesthetically pleasing finish.
  • Better Heat Resistance :Melts at a higher temperature, reducing tunneling and waste.
