Dreamcatchers

Product Description:

Beautifully handcrafted with woven webs, feathers, and beads, dreamcatchers are decorative charms believed to protect against bad dreams. Hanging above a bed or in living spaces, they add a touch of boho elegance while symbolizing positivity, peace, and spiritual protection!

Product Features:

  • Handwoven Web Design:Symbolizes a spider's web to catch bad dreams and let good ones pass through.
  • Decorative Feathers:Traditionally used to guide good dreams gently to the sleeper.
  • Beads & Charms:Add visual appeal and spiritual significance, often representing different elements or energies.
  • Made with Natural Materials: Cotton threads, wooden hoops, and eco-friendly feathers.
  • Available in Various Colors:From calming neutrals to vibrant boho palettes.
  • Multiple Sizes:Mini (3-4"), Medium (6-8"), and Large (10-15"+) for different spaces.
  • Ideal for Home Décor or Gifting:Blends spirituality with aesthetic appeal, perfect for bedrooms, nurseries, or as thoughtful gifts.
