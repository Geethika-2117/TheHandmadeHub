Vinyl record clocks

Product Description:

Vinyl Record Clocks are unique, upcycled timepieces crafted from real vintage vinyl records. These decorative wall clocks blend retro charm with modern functionality, making them perfect statement pieces for music lovers and home décor enthusiasts alike!

Product Features:

  • Upcycled Vinyl Base:Made from authentic, repurposed vinyl records, each clock is eco-friendly and one-of-a-kind.
  • Silent Quartz Movement:Features a noiseless, high-precision quartz mechanism for accurate timekeeping without ticking sounds.
  • Custom Designs:Available in various artistic cutouts and themes such as music bands, movies, cities, and abstract patterns.
  • Lightweight & Durable:Despite being made of vinyl, the clocks are sturdy yet lightweight for easy wall mounting.
  • Easy Installation:Comes with a built-in hook or hanging slot, ready to mount straight out of the box.
  • Battery Operated: Runs on a standard AA battery (not always included), making it wire-free and convenient.
  • Versatile Décor:Adds a vintage or quirky charm to living rooms, studios, bedrooms, or cafes.
